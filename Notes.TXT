-------------------------------------------------------
THE FOLLOWING IS FOR TCP/IP SERVER-CLIENT RELATIONSHIPS
-------------------------------------------------------
Server:
1-Initialize Winsock
2-Create Socket
3-Bind Socket
4-Listen for Client
5-Accept Client connnection
6-Send Receive data
7-Disconnect
Client:
1-Initialize Winsock
2-Create Socket
3-Connect to server
4-Send Receive data
5-Disconnect
